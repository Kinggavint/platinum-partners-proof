Our Story

Built by Practice Owners,
for Practice Owners

Platinum Partners was founded with a singular vision: to help hearing healthcare practice owners break free from the daily grind and build businesses that generate lasting wealth and impact.

After spending years in the trenches running private practices, our founders discovered the patterns that separate thriving businesses from struggling ones. They distilled these insights into a comprehensive coaching program that has now helped hundreds of owners transform their practices.

Today, we're the leading coaching program in the hearing healthcare industry, known for our no-nonsense approach, measurable results, and the powerful community we've built.
